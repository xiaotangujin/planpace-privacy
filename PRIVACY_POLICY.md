# CuePlan Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 6, 2026

CuePlan, named 按部就班 in Simplified Chinese, is an offline-first flow planning app that helps people complete work and daily routines step by step.

## Data We Collect

CuePlan does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with us or third parties.

Your plans, steps, statistics, themes, pet settings, imported flow packages, and running flow state are stored locally on your device. If you export or share a JSON file or QR code, you choose where that data goes.

## Location

CuePlan can use location conditions when you choose location-based flow starts, location-based step endings, or location-based route decisions.

Location is processed on your device through iOS location services and geofencing. CuePlan does not send your location to our servers. CuePlan does not operate a server for location tracking.

You can turn location access off at any time in iOS Settings.

## Camera

CuePlan can use the camera to scan offline flow QR codes. QR scanning happens on your device. Camera images are not uploaded to us.

You can turn camera access off at any time in iOS Settings.

## Notifications and Live Activities

CuePlan can show Live Activities, Dynamic Island status, and optional local notifications so you can see the current step of a running flow. These features use on-device flow information and do not send your plan data to us.

## Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

CuePlan does not use third-party advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, or analytics services. CuePlan does not track you across apps or websites.

## Data Retention

Because CuePlan stores flow data locally, your data remains on your device until you delete it, delete the app, or export it somewhere you choose.

## Children

CuePlan is not designed to collect personal information from children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when CuePlan changes. The updated version will be published at the same URL with a new “Last updated” date.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact us at: guzhoudiaoxue@outlook.com

# 按部就班隐私政策

最后更新：2026 年 6 月 6 日

CuePlan（简体中文名称：按部就班）是一款离线优先的流程计划 App，帮助用户按步骤完成工作和生活中的事项。

## 我们收集的数据

按部就班不会向我们或第三方收集、传输、出售或共享你的个人数据。

你的计划、环节、统计记录、主题、宠物陪伴设置、导入的流程包以及正在运行的流程状态都存储在你的设备本地。如果你导出或分享 JSON 文件、二维码，由你自行决定这些数据发送到哪里。

## 定位

当你主动使用地点开始流程、到达地点结束环节、或基于地点判断计划线路时，按部就班会使用定位条件。

定位通过 iOS 系统定位服务和地理围栏在设备本地处理。按部就班不会把你的位置发送到我们的服务器，也没有用于位置追踪的服务器。

你可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中关闭定位权限。

## 相机

按部就班会使用相机扫描离线流程二维码。二维码识别在设备本地完成，相机图像不会上传给我们。

你可以随时在 iOS 系统设置中关闭相机权限。

## 通知和实时活动

按部就班可以显示实时活动、灵动岛状态和可选的本地通知，用来展示正在运行流程的当前环节。这些功能使用设备本地流程信息，不会把你的计划数据发送给我们。

## 分析、广告和追踪

按部就班不使用第三方广告 SDK、追踪 SDK 或分析服务。按部就班不会跨 App 或网站追踪你。

## 数据保留

由于按部就班的数据存储在设备本地，你的数据会保留在你的设备上，直到你删除数据、删除 App，或主动导出到你选择的位置。

## 儿童

按部就班不会主动收集儿童个人信息。我们不会有意收集任何人的个人信息。

## 政策变更

当按部就班发生变化时，我们可能会更新本隐私政策。更新版本会发布在同一 URL，并更新“最后更新”日期。

## 联系方式

如有隐私相关问题，请通过以下邮箱联系我们：guzhoudiaoxue@outlook.com
